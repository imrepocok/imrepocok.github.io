Business context — subscriber growth

Acquisition collapsed — retention held the business together

The platform grew steadily from 2005 to 2019, peaking at 17,153 new subscribers in 2019. When Covid hit, new acquisition dropped 42% to 9,898 in 2020 and fell further to 7,414 in 2021. Yet total subscriber numbers kept rising — because existing members stayed. During Covid, retention was the only growth engine.

Stay length by travel type

The further you travel, the longer you stay

Intercontinental exchanges average 7.8 nights — 44% longer than domestic stays at 5.4 nights. Members who travel long-haul justify the cost by staying longer. The top 10% of intercontinental exchanges last 15+ nights.
